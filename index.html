The Childhood Immunization E-Registration with Queue Monitoring System

Immunization is essential for keeping our children safe from deadly diseases. Unfortunately, the realities of the current health care systems can make getting a child vaccinated a difficult and time-consuming process. But with the recent development of an innovative e-registration and queue monitoring system, immunizations can be made much easier—both for parents and healthcare professionals.

E-REGISTRATION

With the E-registration feature, parents and guardian of infants and children can now register online using an Android phone. The E-registration system involves creating a digital record of a child's immunizations and making this information available to parents and guardians online. This allows parents to easily check whether their child is up-to-date with their vaccinations, as well as receive reminders for upcoming appointments.

VIRTUAL QUEUE MONITORING

Queue monitoring systems help clinics manage patient flow and reduce waiting times. The parents and guardian can track the queue status virtually. With a virtual queue monitoring system, parents and children can now wait remotely, where they don't have to physically wait in line to get a service.
